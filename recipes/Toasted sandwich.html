Grilled Chicken Salad

Description

Make the perfect,simple, toasted sandwich with plenty of oozy cheese.

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 4 slices of toast bread
  • Cheese in slices
  • Chicken ham
  • Toppings by your own preferences

Steps

  1. Preheat an sandwich toaster.
  2. In a meantime make a sandwich by putting all the ingridients between slices of bread.
  3. Put the sandwich into an toaster for a minute or two.
  4. Voila, enjoy your tasty sandwich.
